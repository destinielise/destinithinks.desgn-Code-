Ora Therapy

service design

Opportunity

My team wanted to improve the health care industry. During discussion, We discovered a shared experience, terrible mental health care service. At a time when people at their lowest, the mental services often come up short.

There is no way to throughly prescreen your therapist

The major pain point we observed was the inability to test therapist compatibility. We began to sketch out a service focused on creating a matching algorithm.

Print Advertising

The visual language of our brand is very important because of the nature of our service. We decided to use landscape scenery to relief some of the tension behind mental heath care.

Keepsakes

We also wanted to tailor he actually therapy session by integrating keepsakes for the therapist to give away. These give positive reinforcement and encourage the patient to continue therapy.

After prototyping, our concept proved successful. However, there are many complexities within the medical billing realm that complicate this industry. In the future, we would consider sensitivity to language, cost, and insurance compatibility.
